Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a task?

Go to your Dashboard and click on "Add Task." Fill in the details and save.

How do I add reminders?

Open the "Reminders" page and click "Add Reminder" to set one for a specific date and time.

How do I chat with EduMate?

Click on "Chat with EduMate" on your Dashboard. You can ask about upcoming tasks or reminders.

Can I edit my profile?

Yes. Go to your Profile page and click "Edit Profile" to update your email or password.

What if I forget my password?

Use the “Forgot Password” link on the login page to reset your account.
